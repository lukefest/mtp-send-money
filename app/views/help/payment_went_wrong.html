Help I have an issue with my payment

Occasionally, something can go wrong with a payment. You may receive an unexpected refund, the prisoner may have not received the money or you may suddenly be unable to use the service. These are the most common errors:

I made a bank transfer but my money was returned

You may have used an incorrect reference. If so, you’ll get a refund within 4 working days of making the bank transfer. Your bank statement will show ‘GBS RE NOMS AGENCY’ and the reference will be ‘REFUND’. The prisoner will not have received the money.

I may have the wrong prisoner details

The prisoner ‘date of birth’ and ‘prisoner number’ must both be correct to make a payment. Check first with the prisoner to make sure you both have the same details. If you do but still can't use the service, ask the prisoner to check with the wing officer that their details are correct on the database.

I made a bank transfer but my payment didn’t go through

When you make a transfer at a bank branch or from an international bank account, your payment won’t go through. That means we can’t credit the prisoner and you’ll have to request a refund.

Suddenly my debit card payments aren’t working

Please contact us

Payment has taken longer than 3 days

Sometimes payment can take longer for various reasons. For example, individual prisons can put a payment on hold or the money could be in the prisoner's account but they don't know yet. If you're still worried, please contact the prison

I haven’t got a confirmation email

If you paid by debit card, please check your spam/junk folder. Otherwise, you may have mis-typed your email address but the payment will still have gone through.

The prisoner has just moved prison

The money will be transferred to the next prison, whether it’s a public or private prison. If it’s a private prison the process may take longer.

I want my money back

Refunds aren’t possible. You can’t cancel a payment once it’s been sent. You only get a refund if you tried to do a bank transfer but used an incorrect reference. However, if the prisoner has been recently released, please contact the prison and request a refund

I'd like a refund

Refunds aren’t possible. You can’t cancel a payment once it’s been sent. You only get a refund if you tried to do a bank transfer but used an incorrect reference. However, if the prisoner has been recently released, please contact the prison and request a refund
