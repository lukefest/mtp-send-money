Help A payment has taken longer

In almost all cases, the payment reaches the prisoner’s bank account in up to 3 days.

However, if it seems to be taking longer, it could be because an individual prison has put a payment on hold or the money could be in the prisoner's account but the prisoner doesn’t know about it yet.

If you’re still worried after 7-10 days, please contact the prison.
