You may have used an incorrect reference. If this is the case, you’ll receive a refund within 4 working days of making the transfer. Your bank statement will show ‘GBS RE NOMS AGENCY’ and the reference will be ‘refund’.

If a payment doesn’t go through

When you make a transfer at a bank branch or from an international bank account, your payment won't go through. That means we can't credit the prisoner and you'll have to request a refund. Please follow these steps.
